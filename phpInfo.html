PHP

Learn about PHP, one of the top scripting languages in the world of web development.

PHP was created in 1994 by Rasmus Lerdorf. It was first created to track visits to a website, and evolved into a highly developed scripting language used in website development. PHP is supported still today, and still widely used on a variety of websites.
Source: https://www.php.net/manual/en/history.php.php
